Frequently Asked Questions

What is the purpose of the app?

PartiXpender is a mobile money-spraying application that helps party-goers to have access to their money in real time without stress.

Is the app available on both iOS and Android?

Yes, the app is available for download on both the Apple App Store for iOS devices and the Google Play Store for Android devices.

How do I get started with the app?

Download the app, create an account, and follow the onboarding process to set up your account, credit your purse, and add ID /scan QR code to spray.

How much commission does the app charge?

PartiXpender app offers two commission charges. 200 naira on every spray below 10k and 500 naira on every 10k sprayed.
